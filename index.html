Welcome

I am an Associate Professor at the Institute of Clinical Pharmacology and Toxicology at Charité. My research focuses on the effectiveness and safety of medications in vulnerable populations, including patients with chronic kidney or liver disease, older adults, as well as patients subjected to polypharmacy and at risk of drug-drug interactions.

Given the underrepresentation of such populations in randomized trials, my research program provides much needed real-world evidence on the benefit-risk profile of drugs. For the implementation of the program, I use multiple data sources including German and Canadian administrative healthcare data and electronic medical records from the United Kingdom. My research is supported by the German Research Foundation, the Canadian Institutes of Health Research, and the Fonds de Recherche du Québec–Santé.
