Lab Team

At C-RWE Lab, we have a diverse team comprising medical doctors, pharmacists, clinical pharmacology residents, epidemiologists, statisticians, and grad students in different curricula. C-RWE Lab is based at the Institute of Clinical Pharmacology and Toxicology at Charité, but our members are spread across different countries and institutions.
